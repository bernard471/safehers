Impact & Outcomes

Measured.
Published. Accountable.

SafeHer Foundation is committed to transparent, evidence-based impact reporting. Every programme is evaluated against clear outcomes, and results are shared openly with our donors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Monitoring & Evaluation

How we measure impact.

01

Baseline Assessment

Pre-programme surveys measure knowledge, confidence, and safety practices before intervention.

02

Programme Delivery

Facilitator observations, attendance tracking, and real-time participant feedback during sessions.

03

Post-Programme Evaluation

Knowledge assessments and confidence surveys measure immediate learning outcomes.

04

Follow-Up Impact

3-month and 12-month follow-up surveys track sustained behaviour change and safety improvements.

What we measure

Core impact indicators.

Knowledge Gain

Percentage increase in safety knowledge scores between pre- and post-programme assessments.

Behaviour Change

Adoption of safety practices (2FA setup, emergency contacts, financial planning) measured at 3-month follow-up.

Confidence & Agency

Self-reported confidence in handling safety threats, measured on a standardised scale.

Incident Reporting

Increase in willingness and ability to report safety incidents to appropriate authorities.

Educator Multiplication

Number of additional women reached by each certified educator within 12 months of certification.

Institutional Adoption

Organisations that embed SafeHers content into their ongoing policies and orientation programmes.
