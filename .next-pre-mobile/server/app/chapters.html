Start a chapter

How to bring SafeHers to your country.

01

Express Interest

Contact us with your country, city, and whether you want a campus or community chapter.

02

Get Trained

Complete chapter leader training (virtual or in-person). Learn the SafeHers curriculum and facilitation methods.

03

Recruit Members

Build your founding group of 10+ members. We provide social media assets and recruitment materials.

04

Launch & Grow

Hold your first workshop, appear on the SafeHers chapter map, and connect with the pan-African network.

Campus Chapters

For universities

  • Chapter leader training (virtual or in-person)
  • SafeHers curriculum for peer workshops
  • Branded identity and social media assets
  • Connection to pan-African chapter network
  • Annual chapter recognition awards
  • Priority access to SafeHer Academy courses

Community Chapters

For communities

  • Subsidised educator certification for chapter leaders
  • Community-appropriate curriculum adaptations
  • Printed materials and visual aids
  • Quarterly check-ins with foundation team
  • Recognition on SafeHers chapters map
  • Support for local events and workshops
