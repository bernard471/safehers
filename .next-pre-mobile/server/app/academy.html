Common questions

About the Academy.

Is SafeHer Academy really free?

Yes. All courses are free for women and girls. Funded by donors and institutional partners. No credit card required, no hidden fees.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. Complete all lessons and pass the quizzes to earn a verifiable SafeHer Foundation certificate with a unique ID that anyone can verify online.

How long do courses take?

Courses range from 3 to 6 hours. You learn at your own pace — there are no deadlines. Pick up where you left off anytime.

Can I access courses on my phone?

Yes. SafeHer Academy is fully responsive. All courses work on phones, tablets, and computers. No app download required.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your data is protected under Ghana's Data Protection Act 2012. Safety plans and consultation records are private and accessible only to you.

Can my school or organisation use this?

Yes. Contact us to set up an institutional partnership with cohort management, progress tracking, and impact reporting.
